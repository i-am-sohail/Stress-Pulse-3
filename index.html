4-7-8 Breathing
When stress spikes: inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s. Repeat 4 times. Directly calms the nervous system. Use the built-in tool in Live Monitor.
⏱️
Pomodoro Technique
Study 25 min, rest 5 min, repeat 4 times, then rest 20 min. Especially effective for JEE/NEET mock test prep — prevents 3-hour burnout sessions.
💧
Hydration & Posture
Dehydration raises stress by 25%. Keep water at your desk. Sit upright — slouching cuts oxygen to brain. Stand 2 minutes every 30 minutes.
🌙
Sleep Over Late Study
Memory consolidates during REM sleep. 8 hours sleep with midnight study beats 5 hours sleep with 2 AM study every time for long-term retention.
✍️
Active Recall
Writing answers from memory is 3× more effective than re-reading. The mild stress of recall is productive — it literally strengthens neural connections.
🔴
When to Stop
If the stress meter stays red for 10+ minutes, end the session. Persistent stress blocks learning. Talk to a parent, teacher, or counselor early.
